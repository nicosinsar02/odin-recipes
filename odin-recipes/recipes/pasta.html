Recipe for Pasta

Description

A simple recipe with ingredients you can find in any grocery store. First, the dough needs to be made and shaped into spaghetti. Following that, the pasta is boiled. While it boils the ground beef is seasoned and cooked. After that, the pasta is added to the meat and the tomato sauce is added.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Eggs
  • Yeast
  • Flour
  • Minced beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Salt
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Make the dough.
  2. Shape it into spaghetti.
  3. Boil the spaghetti.
  4. Season and cook the meat.
  5. Add pasta to the meat.
  6. Add tomato sauce.
