How to Cook a Medium Rare Steak

Description

Simple method to cook steak. First, purchase a good quality cut of beef without too much fat. After that, season it with seasalt and cover it in butter. Then put it on the grill and cook it until it is a perfect medium rare.

Ingredients

  • Thick cut of lean beef
  • Seasalt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Season meat with seasalt.
  2. Cover meat with butter.
  3. Put steak on grill to medium rare.
