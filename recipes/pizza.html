Recipe for Pepperoni Pizza

Description

An easy to follow recipe with simple ingredients you can find in any grocery store. The recipe first starts with making a simple dough. After the dough is made and shaped, tomato sauce, cheese and pepperoni are added.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Flour
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Make the dough.
  2. Add the sauce and spread it around.
  3. Add the cheese, do not overdo it.
  4. Add the pepperoni.
  5. Put it in the oven.
